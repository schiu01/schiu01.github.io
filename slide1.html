Professional Soccer Clubs and World Cup

By Suk Ching Steven, Chiu - July, 2026

CS416 - Data Visualization - Final Assignment / University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign

Every 4 years, the very best soccer players representing their countries meet for a tournament to decide which country has the best team and gets the golden trophy for the next four years.

During the other years, most players play professionally at clubs that may not be located in their own country. European clubs are considered as top-talent of the world.

The Questions:

  • Which regions are represented the most, for world-cup quality players?
  • The top-finalist teams, does any club standout amongst the rest?
This interactive chart will try answering that question.

First - Legend and definitions

  • World Soccer Federation: They are Member Associations of FIFA ( Fédération Internationale de Football Association). The member associations represents the countries around the word and are regionally categorized.
  • Soccer Club Location: Regions where the professional clubs are located.
  • The Chart:
    • Each rectangle around the circle represents one of the 48 countries selected to compete in 2026 Football World Cup
    • Bubble Chart represents the professional clubs and size denotes number of players being represented
    • The lines linking clubs to countries are players belonging to the club and representing the connected country
    • Only Top players are annotated along the player line.
